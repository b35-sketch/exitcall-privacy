Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 April 2025

Exit Call ("the App") is a fake-call simulator. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

Exit Call does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. All caller names, photos and ringtones you create are stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data

On iOS 14.5+, the App requests your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt before any tracking occurs. You can opt out at any time in your device settings.

See Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

Purchases are processed by RevenueCat through Apple App Store or Google Play. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous app user ID
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Device type and OS version

No payment card details are handled by the App. See RevenueCat's policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

4. Device Permissions

The App may request these permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver scheduled fake calls
  • Vibration — to simulate a real incoming call
  • Motion / Accelerometer — for the "Shake to Call" feature (Pro only)

These are used solely within the App and no sensor data is transmitted externally.

5. Local Storage

The App uses your device's local storage to save:

  • Your chosen caller names and photos
  • Custom ringtone files
  • Quick-trigger preferences

This data never leaves your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Exit Call is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
