Privacy Policy - Exit Call

Last updated: March 2026

Exit Call is a fake call simulator app. It operates primarily offline and does not require account creation.

Data We Collect

Exit Call itself does not collect or store any personal data. However, the app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob — Displays ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy.

RevenueCat — Handles in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes purchase transaction data through Apple/Google. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Data Storage

All app settings (caller names, scheduled calls, preferences) are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications — To alert you of scheduled fake calls
  • Vibration — To simulate incoming call vibration

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data from them is collected or shared.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
